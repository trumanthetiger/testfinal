KNEES

Structure

Knees are like hinges that connect your upper and lower legs. They allow you to do cool things like walk, run, and dance the Macarena.

Function

Knees are the MVPs of your legs. They absorb shock when you jump and protect you when you fall(unless you're me). Without knees, you'd be like a flamingo -- but less fabulous.

Development

Knees start off as tiny bumps on your fetal legs, and then they grow and develop into big bumps on your adult legs. It's like a caterpillar turning into a beautiful butterfly, except less pretty and more useful.
