Toes

Structure

Toes are bony digits located at the end of the foot. They come in a variety of shapes and sizes, just like us.

Function

Toes serve a critical role in balance and walking. Thats why we cram them into shoes with narrow toe bases to make them less effective.

Development

Toes develop in the womb just like every other part of the body. Literally all growth is pretty much the same.
